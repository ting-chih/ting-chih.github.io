Curriculum vitæ / Google Scholar

I am a Ph.D. student in CSAIL and Machine Learning group of MIT EECS, under the supervision of Prof. Stefanie Jegelka and Prof. Antonio Torralba. Previously I worked with Prof. Sanja Fidler as a visiting student at University of Toronto and Prof. Min Sun as an undergraduate researcher at National Tsing Hua University, Taiwan. My research mainly focus on deep learning and its applications in Machine Learning and Computer Vision. In particular, I am interested in representation learning, unsupervised learning, and statistical learning theory.
